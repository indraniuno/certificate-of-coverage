Your Policy Information
Your Benefits
Accidental Death and Disability Benefit

100% of your benefit amount is given to your families in case of your death. In case of disability caused by an accident, you will receive a percentage of your benefit amount to help pay for your medical treatment, daily expenses, and etc.

Php 100,000

Double Indemnity Benefit

Get double of your benefit in case of physical injury due to a burning facility, riding in an elevator and/or travelling as a fare paying passenger of a licensed public utility vehicle.

Unprovoked Murder and Assault Benefit (On top of Group Personal Accident)

The benefit amount is given to you or your families in the event of disability or death caused by a robbery, hold-up, assault or other criminal offence.

Php 50,000

Total Permanent Disability Benefit

You will receive lump-sum benefit amount in case of total permanent disability caused by an injury or sickness. This disability has made them unable to do any work and earn money for at least six (6) months. You will receive their benefits in installments. This will help you pay for your medical treatment, daily expenses and etc.

Php 100,000

Daily Hospital Income Benefit

In case of hospital confinement, benefit amount is given to you for your daily financial support. This is provided up 14 days, for each day of hospital confinement.

Php 500.00 per day (Max. of 14 days)

Emergency Care Benefit

Financial support is provided to you for medical expenses due to an emergency. This aids in paying for your treatment, emergency room use, laboratory tests and prescribed medicines.
